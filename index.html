Astrology and Planet

Astrology is the study of celestial influences on human life. Central to astrology are planetary positions, which reveal the location of planets in the zodiac signs and houses at the time of an individual’s birth. These positions shape our personality traits, strengths, challenges, and life events. For instance, the Sun’s position indicates our core identity, while the Moon’s placement reflects our emotions and instincts. By analyzing these planetary arrangements, astrologers offer insights into our cosmic blueprint and guide us toward self-awareness and growth

Birth Chart (Natal Chart):     A birth chart is a snapshot of planetary positions at the moment of an individual’s birth. It’s like a cosmic map. Astrologers analyze the birth chart to understand an individual’s potential, challenges, and life path.

Zodiac Signs and Houses:        The zodiac consists of 12 signs, each associated with specific qualities (cardinal, fixed, or mutable) and elements (fire, earth, air, or water). Planets move through these signs, infusing their energies

Numerology

Numerology is an ancient belief system that suggests a mystical connection between numbers and the events unfolding in the universe. By analyzing numerical values—such as birthdates and letters in names—numerologists unveil hidden meanings and insights into a person’s life, destiny, and personality traits. Whether we calculate single-digit numbers (1 to 9) or explore master numbers the study of numerology offers a fascinating lens through which we can understand our individual path and purpose

Core Numbers:    
Life Path Number: Derived from your birthdate, it represents your life’s purpose and the path you’re meant to follow. It reveals your innate talents and challenges.
Expression Number (Destiny Number): Calculated from your full name, it reflects your potential, talents, and how you express yourself to the world. Soul Urge Number (Heart’s Desire): Based on the vowels in your name, it reveals your inner desires, passions, and emotional needs. Personality Number: Derived from consonants in your name, it describes how others perceive you.

Vastu

Vastu Shastra, originating in ancient India, is a traditional Hindu system of architecture. The term “Vastu” refers to dwellings or houses, while “Shastra” denotes scientific treatises. These ancient texts describe principles of design, layout, measurements, ground preparation, space arrangement, and spatial geometry. Vastu aims to integrate architecture with nature, harmonizing various parts of a structure based on geometric patterns, symmetry, and directional alignments.

Principles of Vastu:    
Directional Alignment: Vastu emphasizes aligning structures with the cardinal directions (north, south, east, west). Each direction has specific energies associated with it.
Remedies and Corrections : If a building violates Vastu principles, remedies can restore balance.
Yantras: Sacred diagrams placed strategically.
Color Therapy: Using specific colors to enhance energy.
Mirrors: Redirecting energy flow.
Crystals and Gemstones: Balancing vibrations.
Vastu Pyramids: Correcting imbalances.
